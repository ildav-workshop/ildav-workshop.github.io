Interactive Labeling and Data Augmentation for Vision

ICCV 2021 Workshop

About

The workshop on Interactive Labeling and Data Augmentation for Vision (ILDAV) wishes to address ways to solve computer vision problems where large quantities of labeled image data may not be readily available. In these cases, it is of paramount importance that datasets of sufficient size can be quickly and cheaply acquired and labeled. Potential solutions to tackle this problem will be discussed, which may include: 1) few-click and interactive data annotation, 2) data augmentation, and/or 3) synthetic data generation.

More broadly, we aim at fostering a collaboration between academia and industry in terms of leveraging machine learning research and human-in-the-loop, interactive labeling to quickly build datasets that will enable the use of powerful deep models in all problems of computer vision.

The workshop topics include (but are not limited to):

  • Interactive and Few-click annotation
  • Data augmentation
  • Synthetic data for training models
  • Weak supervision
  • Human-in-the-loop learning

ILDAV ICCV 2021: Call for Papers

Overview

The goal of this workshop is to foster a collaboration between academia and industry in terms of leveraging machine learning research and human-in-the-loop, interactive labeling to quickly build datasets that will enable the use of powerful deep models in all problems of computer vision.

More specifically, we welcome contributions in the following areas (though this list is not exhaustive):

  • Interactive and Few-click annotation
  • Data augmentation
  • Synthetic data for training models
  • Weak supervision
  • Human-in-the-loop learning
